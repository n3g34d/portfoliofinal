About Me

My name is Jane and I’m currently a computer science-economics student at Brown university. Before college, I knew I wanted to study economics as I thoroughly enjoyed my econ classes in high school. I later developed a passion for CS when I was encouraged by a friend to take a introductory class my sophomore year. Despite initial apprehension, I soon learned to love the department, community, and opportunity for challenge. I decided to pursue a dual concentration shortly after and have never looked back!

After I graduate next year, I plan to work in finance in New York City. I spent the summer interning at a company that I love and I am thrilled to be returning. I look forward to weekend food adventures, runs in Central Park, and the occasional python program I write to analyze my spread sheets!

In my free time I love to run and bike! I’ve been running for 8 years now and am training for the San Diego marathon in June! I’m hoping to run a Boston qualifying time and be racing there in 2024! I bike to perform low impact cross training and hope to explore the world by foot and bike!
